Pepe's Coin Quest

My very first game

Pepe's Coin Quest was the very first project I ever created. At the time, I was fascinated by platformer games, so I naturally wanted my project to be a platformer. Thankfully, my team approved of the idea, and we decided to move forward with it. Working as a group, we had about three weeks to complete the game. The gameplay revolved around climbing a tower while avoiding enemies, finding a hammer, using it to break a box, retrieving a key, and unlocking a door to progress to the next level. Since this was our first project, we didn’t have time to design a second level. However, even with just one level, the game felt rewarding and enjoyable to play.

Unfortunately, I don’t have any proper gameplay footage available at the moment. Since it’s an older game, I deleted it to free up space on my computer—an oversight I now regret. However, I’ll do my best to track down some footage and update this page as soon as I can!

The footage below was recorded on my phone during the development phase. Please note that some bugs and odd movements might appear, but I hope you enjoy it nonetheless!

Takeaways

It’s worth mentioning that in this project, I was responsible for both the movement mechanics and animations. This experience gave me valuable insight into how third-person platformer movement works, as well as a deeper understanding of animation within the Unity engine!
